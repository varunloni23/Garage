Service Details

Oil Change Service

Regular oil changes are crucial for maintaining your engine's health and performance. Our comprehensive oil change service includes:

  • Draining the old oil and replacing the oil filter
  • Filling with high-quality conventional, synthetic blend, or full synthetic oil
  • Checking and topping off all fluids
  • Multi-point inspection of key components
  • Resetting the oil life monitor

We recommend changing your oil every 3,000-5,000 miles for conventional oil, or 7,500-10,000 miles for synthetic oil, depending on your vehicle and driving conditions.

Brake Service

Your vehicle's braking system is its most critical safety feature. Our comprehensive brake service includes:

  • Inspection of brake pads, rotors, calipers, and brake lines
  • Replacement of worn brake pads or shoes
  • Resurfacing or replacement of rotors or drums as needed
  • Brake fluid flush and replacement
  • Adjustment of parking brake

We recommend having your brakes inspected at least once a year or immediately if you notice any warning signs like squealing, grinding, or reduced braking performance.

Engine Tune-up

Keep your engine running smoothly and efficiently with our comprehensive tune-up service:

  • Replacement of spark plugs
  • Inspection and replacement of air and fuel filters
  • Cleaning or replacement of throttle body
  • Inspection of ignition system components
  • Computer diagnostic scan and adjustment
  • Inspection of belts and hoses

We recommend a tune-up every 30,000-100,000 miles, depending on your vehicle's age, make, and model. Regular tune-ups can improve fuel efficiency, reduce emissions, and prevent costly repairs.
