Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a service appointment?

You can schedule a service appointment by calling us directly, using our online scheduling system on our website, or by visiting our garage during business hours.

Do I need an appointment for routine maintenance?

While we do accept walk-ins for certain services, we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure prompt service and minimize wait times.

How often should I change my oil?

For conventional oil, we recommend changes every 3,000-5,000 miles. For synthetic oil, every 7,500-10,000 miles is typical. However, this can vary based on your vehicle's make, model, and driving conditions.

Do you offer warranties on your services?

Yes, we offer warranties on all our repair services and parts. The warranty period varies depending on the service or part, but our staff will provide you with specific warranty information at the time of service.
