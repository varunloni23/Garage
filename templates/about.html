Our Story

Founded in 2005, GarageHub began with a simple mission: to provide honest, high-quality automotive services at fair prices. What started as a small, family-owned garage has grown into a trusted name in automotive care, serving thousands of satisfied customers.

Our founder, Michael Roberts, had spent over 20 years working as a mechanic at various dealerships and repair shops before deciding to open his own business. Frustrated by the industry's focus on quick profits over quality service, he envisioned a garage where transparency, expertise, and customer satisfaction would be the top priorities.

Today, GarageHub continues to uphold these values while embracing modern technology and techniques to provide the best possible service for all types of vehicles.

Our Mission

At GarageHub, our mission is to provide exceptional automotive care through technical excellence, honest communication, and outstanding customer service.

We believe that every customer deserves:

  • Clear explanations of their vehicle's condition
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden fees
  • Quality repairs using the best parts and techniques
  • Respectful service from knowledgeable professionals
  • A commitment to vehicle safety and reliability

We strive to build long-term relationships with our customers by earning their trust through every interaction and service we provide.
